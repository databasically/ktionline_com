Services

Construction Services

Construction Services performed involve all required on-site observation and testing as well as laboratory testing to insure compliance with contract specifications. This includes UBC/BOCA Special Inspections, Resident Engineering Services, sampling, testing and documenting of all required tests. Call or contact us to request proposals. Read more...

Geotechnical

Geotechnical investigations involve the production of geotechnical reports including drilling and sampling in the field, laboratory testing and analysis of retrieved samples, and presentation of the activity and test results. This includes engineering recommendations such as soil and bedrock bearing capacities, foundation types, floor slab and paving parameters. Call or contact us to request proposals. Read more...

Environmental

Environmental Site Assessments research and report information concerning environmental conditions and the potential for the presence of hazardous materials associated with a property. KTI offers Phase I and Phase II Environmental Assessments, environmental compliance assessments, surface/subsurface soil and water evaluations, soil corrosivity, environmental compliance assessments, waste water evaluations and environmental monitoring. Call or contact us to request proposals. Read more...

About KTI

KTI is a full service professional consulting and testing facility, offering a broad spectrum of design through construction phase services to the owner, contractor, and their professional consultants. KTI was founded in 1986 in Knob Noster, Missouri, and is headquartered in Lenexa, Kansas. KTI serves the better part of Kansas and Missouri from its various locations.
