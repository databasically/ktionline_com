About KTI

Brief History

KTI was founded in June of 1986 at Whiteman Air Force Base, Missouri to provide testing services to general contractors working on government contracts at Whiteman Air Force Base, which was expanding to accommodate personnel and technical support facilities for the B-2 Stealth Bomber. Over the past twenty-plus years, as KTI has grown and expanded - including moving the corporate headquarters to the Kansas City area - our focus has continued to be a commitment to excellence. Now, as the premier locally-owned full service professional consulting and testing firm in the Kansas City area, we still remain committed to developing a personal relationship with each of our clients. KTI Values

At KTI, we are committed to providing prompt, quality service at a competitive price while maintaining honesty and integrity in all aspects of the organization. The following four values are at the core of KTI's identity.
Quality: KTI is committed to providing the highest quality service to our clients.
Personal Service: KTI is committed to developing a personal relationship with each client, knowing and understanding your project needs.
Convenience: KTI is committed to making it easier to do business with us.

"Building Quality Relationships since 1986"

KTI is a full service professional consulting and testing facility, offering a broad spectrum of design through construction phase services to the owner, contractor, and their professional consultants. KTI was founded in 1986 in Knob Noster, Missouri, and is headquartered in Lenexa, Kansas. KTI serves the better part of Kansas and Missouri from its various locations.
