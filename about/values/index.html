Values

At KTI, we are committed to providing prompt, quality service at a competitive price while maintaining honesty and integrity in all aspects of the organization. The following four values are at the core of KTI's identity.

Quality

We understand that you don't have the time or money to do your job twice, so we help make sure that you get the finest quality product the first time. Quality is more than an accurate report - it encompasses every aspect of our relationship with our clients. KTI is committed to providing the highest quality service to our clients.

Personal Service

Some testing firms have dozens of offices, and headquarters in far-away cities. KTI is locally owned and operated, and based right here in the Kansas City area. We understand the importance of the human aspect in our relationships with our clients. When you choose to work with KTI, you won't be a just a job number on a report, you will be a name and a face. KTI is committed to developing a personal relationship with each client, knowing and understanding your project needs.

Convenience

We know how hectic work can be for a project manager, and understand that time is a scarce commodity. That is why KTI is constantly seeking ways to save our clients' time. Whether its easier to read reports, or a new web-based report delivery system, KTI is committed to making it easier to do business with us.

Experience

KTI has been in business since 1986, and we have seen the construction industry rapidly evolve over that time. From the very beginning KTI has been committed to hiring the best and brightest. The certified Professional Engineers on staff average over fifteen years in the industry. That is experience that is unsurpassed, and we are committed to maintaining the highest quality staff of professional engineers and field technicians in the Kansas City area.

"Building Quality Relationships since 1986"

KTI is a full service professional consulting and testing facility, offering a broad spectrum of design through construction phase services to the owner, contractor, and their professional consultants. KTI was founded in 1986 in Knob Noster, Missouri, and is headquartered in Lenexa, Kansas. KTI serves the better part of Kansas and Missouri from its various locations.
