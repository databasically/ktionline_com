Careers at KTI

Quality People, Quality Company

At KTI we pride ourselves on quality - quality of people and quality of service to our customers. We believe in and are dedicated to a simple set of values: respect for others, teamwork, creativity, and hard work.

KTI is always searching for new talent. As one of Kansas City's premier engineering consulting firms, we rely on our employees to maintain the quality of our firm. We offer flexible hours, a friendly, relaxed atmosphere, supportive staff, and opportunities for growth and advancement. KTI also provides a conscientious work environment with competitive wages and education reimbursement. KTI is an equal opportunity employer.

Current Openings
Field Engineering Technician I

Kruger Technologies, Inc. (KTI) is seeking qualified individuals to fill a Field Engineering Technician I position in the Kansas City area. This is an exciting opportunity to join a strong, growing company. Technicians are responsible for conducting various job site inspections and tests, job site QC oversight, and project management.

Requirements:

  • Certifications from ACI, ICC or NICET
  • OR four-year Engineering degree and corresponding E.I.T. or P.E. license
  • Experience in Construction Materials Testing and Special Inspection

KTI offers a competitive benefits package and a friendly, professional work environment. Interested applicants please email a resume to careers@ktionline.com. Compensation will be commensurate with experience and certifications.

KTI is a full service professional consulting and testing facility, offering a broad spectrum of design through construction phase services to the owner, contractor, and their professional consultants. KTI was founded in 1986 in Knob Noster, Missouri, and is headquartered in Lenexa, Kansas. KTI serves Kansas and Missouri from its four locations. KTI is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
