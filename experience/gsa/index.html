KTI Partners with GWH+BB

We are pleased to announce that KTI has partnered with Gastinger Walker Harden + BeeTriplett Buck (GWH+BB), who have been awarded a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) by the General Services Administration (GSA) - Public Buildings Service (PBS) under the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009. The BPA contract is for Construction Management as Advisor services (Under Multiple Award Schedule SIN 817-7) for Region 4 Southeast, Region 5 Great Lakes, Region 6 Heartland and Region 7 Greater Southwest. The dark blue in the map below is the geographical zone in the contract is held. GWH+BB will be performing these services from their team offices in Kansas City, Chicago, Fort Worth, Houston and Tampa. KTI will provide services from their headquarters in Lenexa, Kansas. The contract (GS10F-09-LP-A-0009) runs from 2009-2014. For questions or more information, please email us to learn more.


About KTI

KTI is a full service professional consulting and testing facility, offering a broad spectrum of design through construction phase services to the owner, contractor, and their professional consultants. KTI was founded in 1986 in Knob Noster, Missouri, and is headquartered in Lenexa, Kansas. KTI serves the better part of Kansas and Missouri from its various locations.
