Experience

Military/Government


Military construction projects bring with them a unique set of requirements for all parties involved. Whether it is the strict quality control requirements or the security clearance, KTI is prepared for military jobs, because we have been there. KTI was founded at Whiteman AFB and has more experience with projects on military installations than any other testing firm in the Kansas City area. See projects...

 

 

Design/Build


The construction industry has rapidly evolved over the past few decades, and one of the biggest changes has been the emergence of the Design/Build project. Design/Build is when the design and construction aspects of a single project are contracted for with a single entity. That entity can be a General Contractor, Architect or Engineer. KTI has been involved with a variety of design/build projects, and has developed relationships with many of the Architecture/Engineering firms, and General contractors in the Greater Kansas City area. See projects...

 

Education


Educational facilities provide unique challenges during the construction phase, commonly including extensive structural masonry, and a unique combination of steel and structural concrete. KTI's experience with schools includes the construction of brand-new facilities and extensive renovations to existing facilities. KTI has served many school districts in and around the Kansas City Metro. See projects...

 

 

Commercial


KTI has extensive project experience on commercial projects. Commercial construction projects include retail, warehouse, office, and manufacturing facilities. The type of structure in these buildings varies widely, and KTI has experience with cast-in-place, tilt-up, pre-cast and steel-frame buildings. Because KTI is a full-service testing and inspection firm, we have the ability and expertise to provide excellent service on every type of commercial project. See projects...

 

Airports


Airports are unique because of the more stringent requirements for the strength of materials and the frequency of testing and inspection. Special equipment is required for testing the strength of concrete used in runways and taxiways. Because of these aspects, KTI employs technicians that are devoted exclusively to aviation related projects. In the past seven years alone, KTI has participated in construction projects at 38 different airports in Kansas and Missouri, and our experience and capabilities are unsurpassed.
See projects...

 

Infrastructure


Highways, Arterial Roads and Bridges form the backbone of America's economy and lifestyle. Our public transportation systems help reduce the production of greenhouse gases and make our cities a cleaner place to live. KTI has been involved with many infrastructure projects in and around Kansas City. We have leveraged our experience to ensure that our infrastructure systems are built to last. See projects...

 

Public Works

Public Works are the utilities you use every day, and range from water and sewer to natural gas and electricity. The majority of KTI's experience in the public works arena has been with water treatment plants (WTPs). Because of their unique design and crucial function, the importance of timely and accurate quality reporting during the construction phase is critical to the success of the project. See projects...

About KTI

KTI is a full service professional consulting and testing facility, offering a broad spectrum of design through construction phase services to the owner, contractor, and their professional consultants. KTI was founded in 1986 in Knob Noster, Missouri, and is headquartered in Lenexa, Kansas. KTI serves the better part of Kansas and Missouri from its various locations.
