Infrastructure

KCATA Bus Rapid Transit (BRT)


KTI performed materials testing and Special Inspections including verification of soil, testing of concrete and inspection of rebar placement for the project. The project included renovations and improvements to multiple bus terminal locations throughout Kansas City, Missouri.

 

 

KC Power & Light District Streets


KTI provided the geotechnical exploration and construction phase materials testing for KC Live Entertainment District in Kansas City, Missouri. The project consisted of pavement design recommendations and testing of compacted soils and concrete. The KC Live District extends from Truman Road north to 13th Street and from Baltimore Avenue east to Grand Blvd, as well as Main and Walnut streets extending from 13th north to 12th Street.

 

KDOT I-635 Paving


Two-year Resident Inspection project including quality control materials testing and inspection of concrete pavement, cement treated base and laboratory testing for three miles of highway pavement replacement from State Avenue north to the Missouri River in Kansas City, Kansas.

 

 

Truman Road Viaduct Final Design


KTI provided drilling, laboratory testing and geotechnical engineering services for the design of the renovation of this significant bridge structure over the Blue River in Kansas City, Missouri. Key engineering consideration concerned with this project included temporary excavations in granular soil deposits that extended below the water table, as well as the impact of these excavations on adjacent streets and structures.
